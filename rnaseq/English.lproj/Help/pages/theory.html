There is a certain amount of theory concerning the most effective ways to memorize pair sets such as vocabulary.

Dissertations could be (and probably have been) written about it. Doing this on the cheap means managing your own learning curve.

Common sense recommends that you probably should not practice your high-percentage items as often as the less-well recognized ones.

The theory comes in because you also should not entirely stop practicing the ones you know well. The devil, as they say, is in the details.

If I loaded this program up with features to take advantage of that theory, I would probably have to charge you for them. This application is not about theory, but about "Practice, Practice, Practice!" Best we leave well-enough alone!
